## Deviations from Original Plan

### Approved Scope Modification

**Original Plan 9.1-02 Goal:** Remove ALL 4 internal imports (summarizer, detector, sanitizer, fetcher) and route everything through pkg/tldt.

**Approved Modification:** Partial refactor only - route fetcher and detector.Analyze, keep summarizer/detector.PII/sanitizer imports.

**Reason:** Architectural gap identified during planning review:
- `pkg/tldt` does not export PII detection functions (`DetectPII`, `SanitizePII`)
- `pkg/tldt` Sanitize function exists but PII sanitization integration needs Phase 10 APIs
- CLI uses advanced summarizer features (Explainer, MatrixSummarizer interfaces) not fully abstracted in pkg/tldt.Summarize

**Decision:** Proceed with partial refactor to make progress on library adoption while Phase 10 adds the missing PII and advanced summarizer APIs. This follows D-04 principle (atomic commits) but scopes the atomic change appropriately.
